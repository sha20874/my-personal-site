In case of flooding, always prioritize your safety. Here's how you can protect yourself:

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Stay away from electrical sources and floodwaters
  • Do not walk or drive through floodwaters
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials such as food, water, medications, and documents
  • Listen to local authorities for flood warnings and evacuation instructions
  • Ensure your home is protected with flood barriers or sandbags, if possible
  • If you are trapped in a vehicle by floodwaters, stay in the vehicle and call for help
